Welcome to Code Fellows!

Recipe for Success

What You'll Need

- One Computer

- 5 Instructors with a Wealth of Knowledge

- One Saturday Afternoon

- A Bit of Patience

- A Positive Attitude

- Caffeine (optional)

    Preheat your day with a hearty breakfast at home.

    Arrive at Code Fellows between 8 and 9 AM. Ensure all necessary ingredients are ready to go.

    Mix with your peers and instructors.

    Slowly marinate in the new knowledge.

    Carmelize your newfound learnings with the tools to create custom websites.

    Bake for 12 hours with your new friends. Be sure not to over fry your brain.

    If you so choose, sprinkle caffeine throughout the cooking process.

    Embrace the munchies! Eat the free lunch and dinner provided by Code Fellows.

    When your Code is done cooking, remove and let cool for 15 minutes. Repeat for 201, 301, and 401 before serving your success to potential employers.

    Enjoy!

    Let's get codin'!

    "If you can't take the heat, relax and ask for help."
